Video Projection Mapping is an exciting new projection technique that can turn almost any surface into a dynamic video display. 
Specialized software is used to warp and mask the projected image to make it fit perfectly on irregularly shaped screens. 
When done right, the end result is a dynamic projection installation that transcends ordinary video projection.


The workshops were an introduction of working with video as a tool for creating and
transforming spaces: to thinking of video as light, and how you can mask a projection to
project on multiple objects and surfaces within the projector´s projection angle and to
exploit the depth of field in video projectors

Exploring how audiovisual technology can be used to transform, create, expand, amplify and interpret physical spaces.
